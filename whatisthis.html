What is this?

BuffyCalculator features visual calculations to explore the science (and meta science) in the Buffy universe

See all calculators

Want to read full explanations about the science? Visit my blog to read the analysis articles

BuffyCalculator is a tool that collects some visual calculators and demonstrators that investigate and explore science (and metascience) from the Buffyverse.

Science fiction is meant to be fiction - but fictional worlds tend to be consistent within their own laws of universal physics. If we can explore how things work, we can explore the show's universe science, and explore various expects of Physics and math. Win win!

Science, math, and corrections

I am trying to do some research and update my physics-brain, but as with all science and math, I might have mistakes. If you find anything that you think is wrong, please consider submitting an issue on the Github page.
